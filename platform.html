★ Spotlight 01 — Marketing Review

Every ad reviewed.
Every flag cited.

Your team submits marketing materials straight into Qapp. The AI reviews each piece against the SEC Marketing Rule — performance presentation, testimonials, hypotheticals, required disclosures — and returns findings with the exact provision cited, ready for the reviewing principal to approve, reject or annotate.

  • Submission portal for employees — decks, one-pagers, web copy, social posts, multi-attachment submissions
  • AI findings with rule-level citations under Rule 206(4)-1 — nothing generic, everything traceable
  • Approve / reject / annotate workflow with a full review trail for the books-and-records file
  • Pending-review digests to exactly the admins who own marketing — not the whole firm

★ Spotlight 02 — Annual Compliance Review

The 206(4)-7 review,
running all year.

The annual review shouldn't be a December scramble. Qapp's Compliance Testing module runs review cycles across every program area — testing steps, findings, evidence attached as you go — then assembles the year into the annual review memo examiners ask for first.

  • Structured review cycles by program area — personal trading, marketing, custody, best execution and beyond
  • Findings and remediation tracked with evidence attached at the point of testing, not reconstructed later
  • AI-drafted annual review memo assembled from the year's actual testing record, ready for CCO sign-off
  • Cycle-activity notifications keep testing moving quarter by quarter — no year-end pileup
